Our Story

Rooted in
African craft.

Girma Prime was born from a simple belief: that the spaces we inhabit should feel alive. We source pots from local artisans, preserve flowers using traditional glycerine methods, and pack every order with the care of a neighbourhood florist.

Every name in our catalogue carries meaning — Hausa, Swahili, Yoruba — because we believe your plants should carry stories too.
